About me!

Yuuki is currently a Second year undergraduate student at University of Essex, majoring in Biological Sciences including Placement Year. He expects to graduate in June 2023 and is looking for opportunities in the fields of Bioinformatics, Immunology, Molecular and Cellular Biology. Born and raised in America to an Italian mother and Japanese father, he then moved to England where he is now studying a B.S. in Biological Sciences at the University of Essex.
